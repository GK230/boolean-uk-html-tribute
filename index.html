Anna Karenina

Cover page of the first volume of Anna Karenina, Moscow, 1878

Dear Anna Karenina,

I love you, you are my favourite novel, even though I have never read you! However, I have watched you in your various forms: the classic starring Greta Garbo, my favourite starring Sean Bean and Sophie Marceau, and even when you were a modern version set in Australia, I still adored you. You have been described acurtely I belive, as the greatest novel ever written, and somewhat disparagingly, in my opinion, as the ultimate relationship drama.

I am currently trying to appreciate your natural beauty by reading you in Russian, even though it takes me an hour to read about two pages using the Collins Russian-English dictionary app. Nevertheless from the first sentence I managed to decipher, I realised you are actually amazing, just as I always felt you were.

Geeta Kotecha
